## What is Aurum?

**Aurum** is the first non-custodial Bitcoin yield aggregator built on the Stacks blockchain. 

By depositing your sBTC into Aurum, you receive **agBTC** (Aurum Yield Share Tokens), which represent your proportional share of the underlying yield-generating strategies.

### Core Features

#### ⚡ Auto-Routing
Smart contracts instantly allocate your sBTC across top-performing lending and delta-neutral vaults like Zest and Hermetica.

#### 🛡️ Non-Custodial
You hold your own agBTC tokens in your wallet. 100% permissionless and fully auditable on-chain.

#### 📈 Blended APY
Enjoy a competitive blended APY (historically 8-10%) without needing to constantly monitor individual protocols.

#### 💼 Liquid Position
Your agBTC is freely tradable and can be used in other DeFi primitives while generating base yield.
