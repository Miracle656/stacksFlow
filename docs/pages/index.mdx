## What is Aurum?

**Aurum** is the first non-custodial Bitcoin yield aggregator built on the Stacks blockchain. 

By depositing your sBTC into Aurum, you receive **agBTC** (Aurum Yield Share Tokens), which represent your proportional share of the underlying yield-generating strategies.
