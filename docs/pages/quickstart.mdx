## Prerequisites

Before interacting with Aurum, you will need:
1. **A Stacks Wallet** (e.g., [Leather](https://leather.io/) or [Xverse](https://www.xverse.app/))
2. **sBTC** (Stacks Bitcoin) to deposit.
3. A small amount of **STX** to pay for transaction fees.

---

## Depositing sBTC

To mint agBTC and begin earning yield:

### 1. Connect Your Wallet
Navigate to the [Aurum App Dashboard](https://stacks-flow.vercel.app/dashboard) and connect your Stacks wallet in the top right corner.

### 2. Enter Deposit Amount
In the Vault card on the left, select the **Deposit** tab and enter the amount of sBTC you wish to supply. The interface will automatically calculate the estimated amount of agBTC you will receive based on the current exchange rate.

### 3. Confirm Transaction
Click **Deposit sBTC**. Your wallet will prompt you to confirm a smart contract call. Once confirmed and broadcast to the network, wait for the transaction to finalize.

---

## Withdrawing sBTC

You can burn your agBTC at any time to reclaim your underlying sBTC plus the accumulated yield.

> **Warning**  
> Depending on network conditions and underlying protocol liquidity, very large withdrawals may be subject to a brief cooldown period (up to 3 days).

### 1. Navigate to Withdraw
From the Dashboard Vault card, select the **Withdraw** tab.

### 2. Enter agBTC Amount
Input the amount of agBTC you want to burn. The UI will show the exact amount of sBTC you will receive.

### 3. Confirm Transaction
Click **Withdraw agBTC** and sign the transaction with your wallet. Once the transaction confirms, the sBTC will be unlocked in your wallet.
