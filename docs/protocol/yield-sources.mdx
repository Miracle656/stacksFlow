Aurum generates its blended APY by interacting with established, audited DeFi protocols within the Stacks ecosystem.

Currently, Aurum integrates with two primary yield sources: **Zest Protocol** and **Hermetica**.

---

## 1. Zest Protocol (Over-collateralized Lending)

Zest Protocol is the premiere lending platform on Stacks. By supplying sBTC to Zest's lending pools, Aurum earns a variable interest rate paid by borrowers.

- **Risk Profile:** Low to Medium
- **Mechanism:** Borrowers supply over-collateralized assets (like STX) to borrow sBTC. The interest they pay to borrow is passed on to suppliers.
- **Yield Format:** Auto-compounding sBTC

## 2. Hermetica (Delta-Neutral Strategies)

Hermetica offers structured products, particularly delta-neutral vaults that generate yield through funding rates on perpetual exchanges and options selling.

- **Risk Profile:** Medium
- **Mechanism:** Executes market-neutral trading strategies (like cash-and-carry arbitrages) that generate yield regardless of Bitcoin's price direction.
- **Yield Format:** Yield is realized in sBTC.

---

## The Blended APY Advantage

By splitting deposits across both protocols, Aurum achieves a **Blended APY**. 
This provides a smoother return curve—when lending rates on Zest are low, Hermetica's trading strategies often compensate, and vice-versa, giving you the best of both worlds without manual management.
