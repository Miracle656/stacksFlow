## System Overview

Aurum acts as a non-custodial yield aggregation layer sitting above other DeFi protocols on Stacks (like Zest and Hermetica).

When you deposit sBTC into Aurum, the protocol mints **agBTC** tokens to your wallet. Behind the scenes, the smart contracts take that sBTC and automatically deposit it into whitelisted yield-generating strategies.

## The agBTC Vault

The core of the architecture is the **agBTC Vault Contract**. This contract is responsible for:
1. Accepting sBTC from users.
2. Minting the agBTC share tokens.
3. Managing the accounting of how much sBTC each agBTC token is entitled to.
4. Routing the deposited sBTC to the active strategy contracts.

## Rebalancing

To maintain optimal yield and risk distribution, Aurum periodically rebalances its underlying sBTC allocation. Currently, the protocol routes funds based on a predefined ratio (e.g., 50% Zest, 50% Hermetica), but future governance updates will allow dynamic allocation based on real-time APY rates.
